## Overview

Employed an intensive combination of on-the-job training in development, governance, and strategy of various software and data applications including grid orchestration software, DevOps, analytics, and digital user interfaces/experiences.
## Key Commitments

- **Customer Strategy and Segmentation**: Use of research, analysis, and examination of customer Segmentation and Strategy to find and target a range of International Customers.
- **DevOps**: Integrated a theming system that allows easy switching and customization of color palettes, typography, and layout styles using CSS variables and Figma tokens.
- **Multi-Source Integration**: Collaborated closely with development team, reducing critical spark errors to 0. This included providing unit testing with code coverage of 70% for Disruption Prepare data pipelines.
- **Documentation and Usage Guidelines**: Developed comprehensive documentation with Data team to showcase product Vision, usage patterns, and System analysis, ensuring the design system is easy to adopt by a range of customers.

## Technologies Used

- **Confluence and AzureDevOps**: Documentation of foundation of International Disruption Prepare strategy, product development, Product Roadmap, and Product Vision.
- **Java**: For creating and managing data pipeline code.
- **Jenkins-Pipelines**: Data Integration into system and testing.
- **Apache Camel**: Used for Data Pipeline Devlopement and Unit Testing.

## Challenges and Learnings

Working with new software systems that are not industry standard had a learning curve but with great teamwork and open communication these challenges are overcome.
## Outcome

International Disruption Prepare is now actively used and sold across multitude of International Customers. My research, analysis, and examination of product strategy, made complex data and customer empathy the foundation of International Disruption Prepares Product Vision.

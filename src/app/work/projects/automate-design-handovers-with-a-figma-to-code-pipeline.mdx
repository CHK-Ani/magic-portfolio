## Overview

In this project, our group worked on crafting the basis of Sleeplytics Sleeplytics is an all-in-one sleep tracking and management application that connects to smartwatches or gathers user-inputted data to monitor sleep quality, schedule, and related metrics. It provides reports, recommendations, and goal-setting features, our vision is to ensure that at least 60% of users set and attempt sleep goals within the first two weeks, offering a seamless alternative to multiple separate apps.


## Key Components

- **Product Vision**: Developed a long term Strategy and vision for the Sleeplytics product.
- **Feature prioritization and list**: Designing and designating Feature Levels, weighting the importance of understanding the heirarchy and priorization of features in a system.
- **System Design and Context**: Designing system operations with user empathy in high regard as well as business context.

## Resources Used

- **Canva**: For designing Product Strategy & Vision, Product Roadmap, Product Requirement Document (PRD), User Journey Map, Competitive Analysis, and Go-To-Market Strategy.
- **Figma + Canva**: For designing wireframe of product.

## Challenges and Learnings

One of the biggest challenges was The importance of having adequate business context to foresee business goals and target users/markets correctly.
